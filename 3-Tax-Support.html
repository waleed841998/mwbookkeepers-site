Professional Tax Support Services

Overview of Tax Support

MW Bookkeepers provides professional Tax Support services tailored for CPAs, CPA firms, and individual clients requiring accurate, reliable handling of US tax data. Our services focus on the precise transfer and organization of IRS forms including W2, W9, 1098, 1099, 1020, 1020S, 1065, along with schedules A, B, C, D, E, 1, 2, 3, K, K-1.

Service Capabilities

  • Accurate transfer of client tax data into software platforms such as ProConnect, Drake, Lacerte, TurboTax.
  • Compilation of all necessary forms and schedules to facilitate generation of the 1040 form or corporate returns.
  • Data verification and organization to minimize errors and streamline CPA review.
  • Support for both individual taxpayers and businesses of all sizes.
  • Efficient preparation that allows CPAs to focus on analysis, strategy, and filing, rather than manual data entry.

Who Benefits

Our Tax Support is specifically designed to meet the needs of:

  • CPA Firms: Obtain accurate client data preloaded in tax software for faster, error-free processing.
  • Individual Clients: Ensure personal IRS forms and schedules are correctly compiled for submission to tax preparers.
  • Small and Large Businesses: Streamline corporate tax processes, employee payroll forms, and partnership returns.
